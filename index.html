About Us

A few words about Wong’s

Let us introduce ourselves

H.K Wong’s is the first restaurant in the Dartmouth area to be opened by Jane Chen. Her second restaurant, China Town has proven to be an overwhelming success in the Halifax area. Both restaurants exemplify her efforts in the restaurant industry, and make her food experience accessible.

Welcome, a place to dine

Jane, and her staff welcome you to her original restaurant, and wishes to provide you with the best food China has to offer. Order with confidence! H.K Wong’s serves all the favorites dishes, and some great new culinary experiences for you try!
